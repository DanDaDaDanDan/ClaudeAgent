## Final Stress Test

### 1. Would I scroll past this?

**Frame 1 Test**: Giant boot descending from above, filling the screen, "Wait—" text

**Verdict**: NO - This is a pattern interrupt. The brain registers threat/tension and MUST know what happens. The descending shoe is not something you see every day on TikTok. It's visually distinctive.

### 2. Would I watch to the end?

**Curiosity Gap Test**: After the hook, we dive into a crack. The question "What's down there?" is established by second 3.

**Verdict**: YES - This is a must-resolve curiosity gap. You can't scroll away not knowing what's in the crack. The payoff (miniature world) is surprising enough to feel worth the wait.

### 3. Would I comment?

**Engagement Test**: "They're everywhere. Look closer."

**Predicted comments**:
- "Now I'm scared to walk outside"
- "BRO I just checked my driveway"
- "Is this real?" / "It's AI obviously"
- Tags friends
- "Part 2????"
- "The wave broke me"

**Verdict**: YES - Multiple comment triggers. The closing text is provocative. The wave creates emotional response.

### 4. Would I save it?

**Aesthetic Test**: Beautiful underground world, glowing mushrooms, tiny civilization

**Verdict**: YES - The underground world is genuinely beautiful. Save-worthy for "show later" or aesthetic collection.

### 5. Would I share it?

**Share Test**: Is this "you have to see this" content?

**Verdict**: LIKELY - The concept is surprising and the execution (if good) is impressive. People share things that make them look like they found something cool.

## Remaining Micro-Concerns

### 1. "They're everywhere" could be misread
**Risk**: 1% of viewers might think it's about bugs/pests
**Impact**: Negligible
**Action**: None needed - context makes it clear

### 2. Boot brand visibility
**Risk**: If a recognizable brand (Nike swoosh, etc.) is visible, could be distracting
**Impact**: Low
**Action**: Prompts say "worn leather boot" - generic is fine

### 3. Tiny figure wave execution
**Risk**: If the wave motion isn't visible enough in video, emotional beat is lost
**Impact**: Medium if it happens
**Mitigation**: Glow effect helps. If still not working, make figure slightly larger.

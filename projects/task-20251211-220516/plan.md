## Story Concepts (Draft)

### Story 1: "The Curator's Eye"
**Protagonist**: Selen Vareth - An archivist in Veridia's Memory Vault who catalogs citizens' backup memories
**Hook**: She discovers her own memory backup shows her living a completely different life
**Twist 1**: The "other" Selen is hunting her
**Twist 2**: She IS the copy - the original wanted to escape Veridia and needed a distraction
**Theme**: You are not your memories; you are your choices
**Ending**: She lets the original escape, choosing to become someone new rather than reclaim the old self

### Story 2: "Symbiont's Gambit"
**Protagonist**: Castor Venn - A border guard who's been implanted with a symbiotic creature from the Ex-Zone
**Hook**: The symbiont gives him visions of the future, making him invaluable to Veridia
**Twist 1**: The visions are actually the symbiont's memories of a previous Loop cycle
**Twist 2**: Veridia has been using symbionts to perpetuate the Loop - his "rebellion" is scripted
**Theme**: True freedom might mean accepting uncertainty rather than seeking control
**Ending**: He chooses to act randomly, breaking the deterministic cycle, accepting he might fail

## Checkpoints

1. After each story outline: Does the twist work? Is it earned?
2. After each full script: Does pacing serve the story? Are silent beats used effectively?
3. After each critique: What specific improvements needed?
4. Before verification: Do both stories hit ALL criteria?
